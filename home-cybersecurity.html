Why Home Cybersecurity Matters

Home networks are no longer simple Wi-Fi and a laptop. Today they are full of smart TVs, phones, game consoles, smart speakers, security cameras, and work-from-home devices. Attackers know this and actively target home users—especially seniors, families, and remote workers— with scams, phishing emails, and malicious websites.

Pitch Black Cyber provides a managed home cybersecurity service that adds a dedicated network firewall appliance to your home and includes ongoing monitoring, protection, and support. You get peace of mind knowing someone is watching your digital front door.

  • Blocks known scam, phishing, and malicious websites
  • Helps protect elderly family members from online fraud
  • Isolates smart devices from your personal computers and phones
  • Includes a real person you can contact before clicking on something suspicious

How the Service Works

  1. Install: We set up a small firewall appliance on your home network—usually in under an hour.
  2. Harden: We secure your Wi-Fi, set up DNS filtering, and isolate risky IoT devices.
  3. Monitor: We keep an eye on alerts for suspicious activity and blocked threats.
  4. Support: You contact us if you get a strange email, text, or pop-up. We help you decide what’s safe.

Everything is designed to be as hands-off as possible for you—no technical knowledge required.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I have to change my internet provider?

No. The firewall appliance works with most common home internet providers and routers. We simply connect it to your existing equipment.

Will this slow down my internet?

For most homes, the impact is minimal and often unnoticeable. We’ll recommend the right hardware model based on your connection speed.

What happens if something breaks?

If you lose connectivity or see something strange, you contact us and we’ll walk you through a fix or adjust the configuration remotely.

Can you help my parents or grandparents even if they live in another city?

Yes. As long as they have an internet connection and we can ship or install the device, we can manage their protection remotely.
